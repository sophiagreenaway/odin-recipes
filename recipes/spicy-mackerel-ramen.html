Spicy Mackerel Ramen

With a short prep time this instant ramen pick-me-up is the perfect afternoon lunch or late night supper. Let's get into it!

Ingredients:

  • 1 packet Shin Ramyun noodles
  • 1.5 tbsp butter
  • 1 4.05 oz can of mackerel, in oil or in brine
  • 2 oz of broccolini
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Red pepper flakes
  • Water

Steps:

  1. Heat 1/2 a tablespoon of butter in a skillet over medium heat. Add a pinch of red pepper flakes and cook for 30 seconds to infuse the butter with flavor.
  2. Add the broccolini and a little salt. Cook, tossing occasionally for 2 minutes.
  3. You will now need to create steam in the pan to help the broccolini soften. Add a couple tablespoons of water and cover the pan.
  4. Reduce the heat to medium low and cook, covered, for 3-5 minutes or until the vegetables are tender and bright green, then remove from the heat.
  5. Cook ramen according to package instructions. Add the flavor packet. Discard the dried vegetable packet.
  6. Add 1 tbsp of cold butter to the noodles in the broth. Stir and let it emulsify.
  7. Transfer your noodles to a bowl. Top with your broccolini and the canned mackerel.
  8. Enjoy!
