Curry Chicken

A classic, spiced caribbean chicken dish. This particular recipe is specific to the Trinidadian locale, but variations are abundant. Without further ado, let's get into it!

Ingredients:

  • 3lbs skinless chicken thighs
  • 1/2 cup green seasoning
  • 1/2 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp pepper
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1/8 cup garlic, chopped
  • 1 chopped habanero, unseeded
  • 4 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala powder
  • 3 tbsp green seasoning
  • 3-4 tbsp oil
  • 2 potatoes, cut in cubes
  • Salt to taste

Steps:

  1. Mix the chicken with the 1/2 cup green seasoning, 1/2 tsp cumin, 1/2 tsp curry powder, 1 tsp salt, and 1 tsp pepper. Let marinate for 2 hours or overnight.
  2. Mix the remaining curry powder, cumin, turmeric, and garam masala in a bowl.
  3. Heat oil over medium high heat and stir in onion, garlic, and chopped habanero and saut√© until the onion turns golden brown.
  4. Add in the mixed spices and stir continuously for several minutes. Add a 1/4 cup of water and make a slurry and cook the curry until it begins to thicken.
  5. Add in the marinated chicken. Mix well and cook covered for 10 minutes, when the chicken begins to simmer in its juices.
  6. Stir and cook for an additional 5-8 minutes, and add 2 cups of water along with the potatoes, salt, green seasoning and cooked covered until the chicken begins to fall off the bone.
  7. Enjoy! Best served with rice.
